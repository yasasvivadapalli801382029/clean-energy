Topic Summary

Geothermal energy innovations are reshaping the global energy landscape by advancing sustainable technologies that reduce carbon emissions and reliance on fossil fuels. Recent innovations in geothermal energy focus on enhancing the efficiency and scalability of geothermal systems, making them a viable alternative for large-scale energy production. One notable breakthrough is the development of enhanced geothermal systems (EGS), which allow for geothermal energy extraction in regions where traditional geothermal resources are not readily available. Another exciting development is the use of geothermal heat pumps for residential and commercial heating and cooling, providing a cost-effective and energy-efficient solution. Furthermore, geothermal power plants are becoming more efficient through advancements in drilling technology, which allow access to deeper and hotter reservoirs. Geothermal energy storage solutions are also being explored, offering potential for reliable, 24/7 clean energy, complementing other renewable sources such as solar and wind.

Technical Aspects

The project uses the following tech stack:

  • Frontend: Angular with TypeScript for creating interactive UI and integrating D3.js for data visualization.
  • Backend: Spring Boot and Java to build a RESTful API that serves sales data.
  • Database: MySQL managed in a Docker container for reliable and scalable storage.
  • Deployment: The application is hosted on an AWS EC2 instance, ensuring scalability and high availability.

The integration of these technologies showcases a robust full-stack solution, from data handling to user interaction, with a focus on modularity and scalability.
